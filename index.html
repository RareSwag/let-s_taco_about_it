About Us

We here at Let's Taco About it love cooking in our makeshift taco stand.We stand for a recount of the 2016 election. Our prices are cheep just like our workers pay. Come try our Lucho Queso on Thursday, or if your feeling a bit hungry come in on Saturday and try our Macho Man Kiler Keemstar. If you come in today and tell the cashier I love Tacoing about it you can get 50% off your purchase but we only do this once sadly.

Our History

We started up in Pittsburg in 1995 with only 4 worker. We stayed there and got More customers and ended up needing 20 workers, and then Trump was elected so we moved up to Nipawin, Canada, We kept most of our loyal cutomers but we ended up doing even better in Canada , Because we have a monopoly on the mexican food in this town. Now we are planning on moving into a normal building and out of the cramped taco stand.
